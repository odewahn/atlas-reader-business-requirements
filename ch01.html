Atlas Reader

This document describes the business requirements documents for the “Atlas Reader” project. The key elements in the system are:

  • The Central Content Store (CCS).  A central storage location for content (HTMLBook or EPUB) that will be served by the Atlas Reader or Full Content Search.  Assuming this is implemented with AWS, there is no development required.
  • Atlas Web Publisher.  An API/UI for loading Atlas HTML Builds or EPUBs into the CCS.  The Web Publisher also provide a light interface for metadata.
  • Atlas Reader.  The gateway app that serves content from the CCS.  There are two main paths: whitelisted and gated.  Whitelisted paths expose content to search indexes, like Google or the internal full content search project.  Gated access serves content to users based on their permissions in the Ownership Service.  The reader can display either HTMLBook (HTML5) or EPUB (to support partner publisher who do not use HTMLBook or Atlas).
