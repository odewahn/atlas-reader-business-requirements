Feature Description

FEATURE DESCRIPTION TYPICALLY CONSISTS OF AN EXPLANATORY PARAGRAPH OR TWO

AND ENDS WITH A BULLET LIST LIKE THIS:

  • Ability to render HTML5 and EPUB (via a tool like epub.js)
  • Ability to browse these projects with beautiful splash pages
  • Ability for Google to index all of the content in the reader 
  • Ability to show preview for users coming from Google based on their first click free program.
  • Ability to log in with Janrain
  • Ability to allow/disallow access based on O'Reilly ownership service API

Non Requirements include:

  • PDF rendering.  PDF will be extermely challenging to render, so we will not natively display PDF in the reader.  Instead, we will convert PDF to HTMLBook.  The reader will still be able to get the original PDF, but the reader will aid in discovery and findability.
  • Paragraph-level annotations.  Data indicates that the paragraph level commenting in Chimera was not worth the time and effort spent developing them.  Instead, we will seek alternative discussion frameworks, either through Disqus or other annotation plug-ins. 
  • Uniform styling for EPUB.  Because we cannot control the source markup provided by distribution clients, we cannot develop uniform stylesheets against their markup.  Consequently, our best option is to render content in the style they provide.
